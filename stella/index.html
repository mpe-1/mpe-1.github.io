This recepie is for making 4 slices.

First take white bread. Preferably a bit dry bread.

Next take 2 eggs and crack them into a bowl.

Add 0,5 dl milk and 0,5 dl cream

Take a small pinch of salt, 3 pinches of vanilla sugar and a small pinch of cinamon.

Then mix!

Take your slices of bread and dip them into the mixture.

Pan fry the bread in butter until it is a golden brown.
